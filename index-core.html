    Vice Chancellor's Message

    Honâ€™ble Prime Minister of India announced establishment of the Horticultural University in Haryana during the Budget Session 2014-15 and Maharana Pratap Horticultural University (MHU) came into existence by Haryana Act No.32 of 2016 on 28th November, 2016. The University is located on the National Highway No. 1, Karnal and is 142 kms from Delhi and well connected with New Bus Stand at Karnal.
    MHU is the 1st Horticulture University in Haryana and the 5th University in the Country. At present, it has five Regional Research Centres and one College of Horticulture at Anjanthali, Karnal where 111 students in Under-graduate and Post-graduate Programmes are currently studying.
