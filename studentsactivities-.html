    STUDENTS ACTIVITIES

    NSS

    National Service Scheme(NSS) activities of the college.

    National Service Scheme, popularly known as NSS under the aegis of Ministry of Youth Affairs & Sports, Govt. of India,  is an extension of activities to the higher education system to orient the student youth to community service while they are studying in education institutions,. Under NSS, students performed various activities like.

    • Good Governance Day celebration:
      Good Governance Day  was virtually celebrated by the students of BSc 1st Year, COH, MHU Karnal. This day  is observed in India annually on the twenty-fifth day of December, the birth anniversary of former-Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee. Good Governance Day was established in 2014 to honor Prime Minister Vajpayee by fostering awareness among the Indian people of accountability in government. Students showed active participation by performing various activities like poster making, essay writing, slogan writing etc.
    • Republic Day Celebration :
      72th Republic Day was celebrated by the students of BSc 1st Year, COH, MHU Karnal by planting tree at their nearby community places with the spirit of developing eco-friendly relationship. This Day honors the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect on 26 January 1950 replacing the Government of India Act (1935) as governing document of India and thus, turning the nation into newly formed republic.

    • NSS Activty EARTH DAY, April 22, 2021

      Earth Day is an annual event held on 22 April to demonstrate support for environmental protection.

    • NSS ACTIVITY

      INTERNATIONAL YEAR OF FRUITS AND VEGETABLES 2021

      The Year 2021 is the UN’s International Year of Fruits and Vegetables (IYFV). It is dedicated to raising awareness about the important role of fruits and vegetables in human nutrition, food security and health.

    Virtual tour to temperate fruit orchards at SKAUST-Kashmir

    On dated 15/06/2021 Virtual tour to temperate fruit orchards at SKAUST-Kashmir was organized in a view to get exposure of the students to the temperate fruit orchards. As in the second semester of the academic year 2020-21 we are teaching a course entitled “Temperate Fruit Crops (FSC-121). This course requires identification of temperate fruits but physical visit/tour of the students is not possible due to Covid-19 pandemic situation. Therefore, virtual tour of the students was organized in which Dr. Amit Kumar (Assistant Professor, Fruit Science) made sincere efforts to make virtual tour successful.                

                 

    One Day Workshop on “Confidence building and stress management”

    On dated 18th June, 2021 CoH, MHU, Karnal organized one day workshop on “ Confidence Building & Stress Management”  through virtual mode. All the students of BSc  (Hons.) Horticulture  1st Year actively participated in the workshop. With the help of various online activities and audio visuals aids , Ms. Manjula conducted workshop and covered various aspects like SWOT Analysis, EGO States, Prioritizing etc.

       

      Poster Making and Slogan Writing competition

    On the occasion of International Yoga Day, ie. 21st June, 2021, CoH, MHU Karnal organized “Poster Making and Slogan Writing competition” through virtual mode for the students of BSc.(Hons.) Horticulture. In the Poster Making and Slogan Writing competition, 14 & 12 students participated respectively. Students were judged on the basis of overall appearance, theme oriented approach , with  time limit, colour combination and layout design. Competition was judged by Dr. Nasib Singh and Dr. A.K.Rohila.  In the Poster making competition Dimple, Jyoti and Jyoti  and in slogan writing competition Abhishek, Paras and Simran secured 1st, 2nd and 3rd postion respectively.
