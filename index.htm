    Vice Chancellor's Message

    The Honourable Prime Minister of India Shri Narender Modi announced for establishment of Horticultural University in Haryana during the budget session of 2014-15. Honourable Union Agriculture & Farmers Welfare Minister, Shri Radha Mohan Singh and the Chief Minister of Haryana Shri Manohar Lal laid the foundation stone at Anjanthali Village Distt. Karnal in the presences of Shri Om Parkash Dhankar the Minister of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare Haryana on 6th April 2016.
