Course - D.El.Ed.

D.El.Ed is a Diploma course in Elementary Education. It is a 2-year full-time diploma course to train teachers for the primary level and can be done with classroom training or distance learning. It is a 4-semester course to prepare students to work in primary/ upper primary schools in different states of the country. The design of the course is such that it includes practical training and internships along with classroom training. It includes principles of child development, education practice, and the role of the teacher in grooming the students.

Eligibility for D.El.Ed 

The candidate should possess good communication skills, patience, creative thinking and an aptitude to teaching. 

It is a course for undergraduates who have completed 10+2 from a recognized board with 50% minimum marks. 

The age limit might vary from one state board to another. However, broadly the minimum age is 18 years and maximum age limit is 35 years. One can check the respective state boards requirements before applying. 

D.El.Ed Admission Process and Entrance Exams

Admission process consists of an entrance exam followed by an interview, if the candidate clears the entrance exam. Entrance tests are conducted on an annual basis by state boards for admission to this course.  Admission is granted to the candidate, based on the marks obtained in the entrance exam, the performance of the candidate in the interview.

Fees range from Rs 5000 to Rs 200,000.

D.El.Ed Syllabus

The syllabus of the Diploma course broadly covers the below listed topics

  1. Childhood and the development of children 
  2. Contemporary society
  3. Education Society
  4. Towards Understanding the self
  5. Pedagogy of English language
  6. Mathematics education for the primary
  7. Proficiency in English
  8. Work and education
  9. Cognition sociocultural context
  10. Teacher identity and school culture
  11. Leadership and change
  12. Pedagogy of environmental studies
  13. Diversity and Education
  14. School Health and education
  15. Fine arts and Education

FAQs regarding Diploma in Elementary Education

Q. What are the minimum eligibility requirements for a diploma in elementary education?

A. All those who have completed 10+2 from a recognized board with 50% minimum marks will be able to apply for D EI Ed. The minimum age is 18 years and the maximum age limit is 35 years.

Q. Is D El Ed equal to B.Ed?

A. No, they are not equivalent.

Q. What can I do after Diploma in Elementary Education?

A. Candidates would be able to get jobs in private schools in their region. To get a job in government schools as an Elementary teacher, candidates will have to appear for the Teacher Recruitment Exam (TRT) conducted by the respective state government.

Q. What is the average fee for D El Ed?

A. The average fee ranges from Rs 5000 to Rs 200,000.

Q. How to get admission into D EI Ed?

A. Admission into Diploma in Elementary Education is mainly based on the entrance exam conducted by various institutes. Many institutions also offer merit-based admission.

Q. From where can I pursue D EI Ed?

A. Many institutions offer D EI Ed including NIOS, Jamia Millia Islamia University, Amity University, etc.

Q. What is the total course duration for Diploma in Elementary Education?

A. D EI Ed is offered for a total of 2 years, divided into 4 semesters.

Q. What is the difference between B. Ed and D EI Ed?

A. While B. Ed qualifies the aspirants to become school teachers up to Class 12, D.Ed qualifies aspirants to become nursery teachers.

Q. Can I do D EI Ed after 12th?

A. Yes, students can pursue the diploma course right after clearing Class 12.

Q. Is D EI Ed a professional course?

A. Yes, D EI Ed is a professional course that prepares candidates to become teachers of primary and upper primary classes.

Q. What is the salary of D El Ed teachers?

A. The per month salary ranges from Rs 5,000 to Rs 60,000.
